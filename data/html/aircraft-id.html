Entering Aircraft Callsigns

When asked for an aircraft callsign, two ways exist to provide this. Both of these can also be applied to indicate airports or waypoints.

Callsign text

Enter the callsign of the aircraft manually. Callsigns of existing aircraft are displayed next to the aircraft icon on the display.

Clicking the aircraft

When clicking a simulated aircraft in the screen with the mouse, the aircraft callsign is copied to the command line.
