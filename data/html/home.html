BlueSky - The Open Air Traffic Simulator

BlueSky is meant as a tool to perform research on Air Traffic Management and Air Traffic Flows. Its goal is to provide everybody who wants to visualize, analyze or simulate air traffic with a tool to do so without any restrictions, licenses or limitations. It can be copied, modified, cited, etc. without any limitations.

This wiki provides (will provide) the documentation for installing, running, and modifying BlueSky.
